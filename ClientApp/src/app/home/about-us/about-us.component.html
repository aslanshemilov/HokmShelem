About Us

Our Mission

At HokmShelem, our mission is to provide a fun and engaging online card game experience for players of all ages. We aim to create a welcoming community where players can connect, compete, and improve their card game skills.

Our Story

HokmShelem was founded in 2024 by a group of card game enthusiasts who wanted to bring the joy of traditional card games to the digital world. Our team is passionate about game design and is dedicated to creating a platform that is both user-friendly and entertaining.
