Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

Welcome to HokmShelem! This policy page outlines the rules and guidelines for using our online card game platform. By using this site, you fully agree to comply with these policies. Please read them carefully.

2. Registration

2.1 Eligibility

  • Users must be at least 15 years old to register.

2.2 Account Creation

  • Users must provide accurate and complete information during registration.
  • Each user is allowed to create only one account.
  • Users are responsible for maintaining the confidentiality of their account information.

3. Profile Creation

3.1 Profile Information

  • Users must provide truthful information in their profiles.
  • Profile pictures and usernames must not contain offensive or inappropriate content.

3.2 Privacy Settings

  • Users can adjust privacy settings to control who can view their profile information.
  • Users should not share personal information (e.g., phone numbers, addresses) publicly.

4. Chatting

4.1 Code of Conduct

  • Be respectful and courteous to all users.
  • Harassment, hate speech, and any form of discrimination will not be tolerated.
  • Do not use offensive, abusive, or inappropriate language.

4.2 Moderation

  • Our team monitors chats to ensure compliance with these policies.
  • Users can report inappropriate behavior to our moderators.

5. Playing Card Games

5.1 Fair Play

  • Users must play fairly and not engage in cheating or hacking.
  • Exploiting game glitches or bugs is prohibited.

5.2 Game Etiquette

  • Be a good sport whether you win or lose.
  • Follow the rules of each game and respect other players.

6. Security

6.1 Account Security

  • Users are responsible for securing their accounts and not sharing login information with others.
  • Report any suspicious activity or security breaches immediately.

6.2 Data Protection

  • We take measures to protect user data and privacy.
  • Refer to our Privacy Policy for more details on how we handle user data.

7. Reporting Violations

7.1 Reporting Mechanism

  • Users can report violations of these policies through the reporting tool provided on the platform.
  • Our team will review and take appropriate action on reported violations.

8. Consequences of Violations

8.1 Disciplinary Actions

  • Violations of these policies may result in warnings, temporary bans, or permanent account suspension.
  • Severe violations will be dealt with more strictly.

9. Changes to Policies

9.1 Policy Updates

  • We may update these policies from time to time.
  • Users will be notified of any significant changes.
